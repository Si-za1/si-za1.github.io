About Me

Who I Am

I'm someone who finds beauty in the in-between moments like the steam rising from a morning pour-over, the way afternoon light hits my journal pages, the quiet satisfaction of creating something meaningful.

By day, I navigate the digital world. By heart, I'm a documenter of small joys and slow living advocate.

What I Love

Analog rituals in a digital age. The weight of a good pen. The smell of freshly ground coffee. The meditative process of arranging thoughts into words. Creating digital spaces that feel warm, human, and intentional.

Why I Create

I believe the internet needs more quiet corners and spaces that feel like a deep breath rather than a shout. I create to document my journey, share what I learn, and hopefully inspire others to embrace their own creative rhythm.

‚òï My Coffee Journey

It started with instant coffee since childhood, evolved through a French press phase, and now I'm deep in the rabbit hole of pour-overs and single origins. Each cup is a small ceremony and a moment for a pause; grinding beans, watching the bloom, waiting for that first sip. Coffee is my creative ritual.
